Hello! This is the creator of the app speaking.
This tool is designed to help UW Tacoma Students and Tacoma residents figure out if they are eligable for the CMC internet or computer subsidy based on your location.
All you have to do is put in or physically click your address or a nearby location , and the app will tell you if you are eligible in the bottom right.
If you have any issues with the app or need help feel free to email me at daelengates@gmail.com
